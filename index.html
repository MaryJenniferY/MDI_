About

Management Development Institute (MDI) is located in the Murshidabad district of West Bengal, India.It was inaugurated by the then President of India, Shri Pranab Mukherjee, on the 24th August 2014. It has accreditation from AICTE.

It offers a full-time PGPM program and other part-time programs (MDP and online certificate courses). It's campus is laid over 10 acres of land. Hostel facilities are provided to its students. Additionally facilities on the campus include an Auditorium, Gym, Sport Facilities, Library, Computer Labs, Canteen, Medical Facilities, and Swimming Pool.

Placements

Placement Process

MDIM's placement process is student-driven. The concerned department contacts the prearranged companies which act as a kick-starter for the entire procedure of placement.
The conventional matters adopted for each of the following processes are:

Company members interact with students on campus via Business Thought Leadership Sessions, Guest Lectures, Live Projects, and other Corporate Forums

MDIM shares it's student's CVs, his/her profiles, and other relevant details with companies.

Pre-Placement Talks are conducted by companies to give complete understanding about the potential future of the roles and positions including their career growth.

Applications and resumes of interested students are shared with the companies.

Companies are ranked by interested students on variables that are essential to their growth and opportunities.

The selection process is conducted by companies which involved shortlisting of CVs, GD & PI session.
